SMS Booking Guide

FarmConnect allows you to book machinery and labour via SMS. Send your booking SMS to FARMCON (3276266).

Machinery Booking

  • Format: BOOK [MACHINERY] [DATE] [LOCATION]
  • Example: BOOK TRACTOR 2024-12-15 KALYANI

Labour Booking

  • Format: LABOR [SKILL] [QUANTITY] [DATE]
  • Example: LABOR HARVESTING 5 2024-12-20

Supported Machinery Types

  • TRACTOR, HARVESTER, PLOUGH, CULTIVATOR, IRRIGATION, SPRAYER, DRONE

Supported Labour Skills

  • HARVESTING, PLANTING, IRRIGATION, SPRAYING, TRACTOR, ORGANIC

Confirmation

After sending your SMS, you will receive a confirmation reply with booking details and contact info.

Help

Send HELP to receive instructions via SMS.
