Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FarmConnect

How do I register on FarmConnect?

Registration is simple! Click on the "Register" button at the top right corner, select your user type (Farmer, Machinery Owner, or Labourer), fill in your details, and verify your phone number. Your account will be activated immediately.

What types of machinery can I find on FarmConnect?

We have a wide range of agricultural machinery including tractors, harvesters, rotavators, ploughs, irrigation systems, sprayers, tillers, balers, and more. You can filter by type, power, location, and price range.

How are the labourers verified on your platform?

All labourers undergo a verification process that includes identity proof, skill certification checks, and background verification. We also have a rating system where farmers can review their experience.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept multiple payment options including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash payments. For online payments, we use secure payment gateways with SSL encryption.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled date without any charges. For cancellations within 24 hours, a nominal fee may apply depending on the service provider's policy.
