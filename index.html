Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on FarmConnect?

Registration is simple! Click on the "Register" button at the top of the page, fill in your details, and verify your phone number. You'll be ready to book in minutes.

What types of machinery can I rent?

We offer a wide range of agricultural equipment including tractors, harvesters, ploughs, seeders, irrigation systems, and more specialized farming tools.

How does the SMS booking work?

Simply send an SMS with your booking request to FARMCON (3276266). Use simple commands like "BOOK TRACTOR [date]" or "FIND LABOR [type]". Our system will process your request and send confirmation.

What if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel bookings through your dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by provider, so please check the specific terms before confirming your booking.

How are payments processed for SMS bookings?

For SMS bookings, payments can be made in cash directly to the service provider. We also support payment via UPI for those who have digital payment access.

Is the SMS service available in regional languages?

Yes! Our SMS service supports Hindi, Punjabi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, and Marathi. Simply start your message with the language code (e.g., "HI" for Hindi).
